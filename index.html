Our Bus

Why SuperBus?

  • 13% of European household’s budget is spent on average on transport.
  • 1h30 is the average time spent per day on transport in Ile-de-France. In Germany, 37% of the active population spent more than 1 hour in transport from home to work. In London 20% of commuters spend more than 2 hours a day traveling to and from work, which adds up to one working day a week.
  • “25% of people are working during their journey from home to work” (Keolis)
  • Meanwhile, women take 3 hours on average per week to do the shopping. For men, that’s 2 hours and half.

Data around the world shows that people, especially those in big cities, spend a very long time on transport every day. We want to invent a new way of looking at transport so that the previous time on the transport can be spent more efficiently.

Our Solution

Our bus does not only transport people, but it is also an extension of work place or home. We want to transform the boring and painful traveling experience into a joyful one.

How It Works

Breakfast Service

Having a proper breakfast is a good start of the day. However, not everyone have this luxury every day because they need to catch a bus/metro or they need more sleep. We provide tasty and healthy breakfast on the bus so people can always have a great start of their day.

Laundry service

Doing laundry after work is a real time-consuming activity and most people hate it. But in our bus, we make it no more a problem. Every morning, the employees just need to pick up the dirty clothes and leave them in the bus’ laundry collection box. Our staff will collect all the laundry and send them to the laundry company. When the employees take the bus back home that day, they can have all their clothes clean and well-ironed.

Grocery Shopping

Employees usually need to buy products for daily use after work. To save time, they can do shopping while on our bus. We provide popular items, such as vegetables, meat, milk, bread, fruit, etc. Moreover, we collaborate with suppliers around big city.

Entertainment

Comfortable chairs and entertainment devices such as tablets and wifi will be provided to make the journey more enjoyable.
